Your brokerage shows you returns. It never tells you why you're making them — or losing them.

Behavioral Alpha is a six-agent AI system that analyzes your personal trading history and produces a behavioral edge report — a direct, quantified answer to where your returns come from, which patterns are costing you alpha, and whether your track record reflects skill or luck.

It is built on free data, open source tooling, and runs entirely on your own machine. The total infrastructure cost is zero.

---

## What It Does

Upload a CSV export from your brokerage. The system enriches every trade with sector, market cap, VIX regime, macro context, and earnings proximity data — then runs six specialized agents in parallel against the full history.

**Behavior agent** — Identifies disposition effect, averaging-down patterns, and panic selling. Measures whether you hold winners longer or shorter than losers and quantifies the cost either way.

**Timing agent** — Finds your optimal hold window, measures win rate around earnings announcements, and identifies day-of-week patterns in exits.

**Regime agent** — Breaks win rate and P&L down by VIX environment and market trend separately. Answers whether your edge holds up when conditions deteriorate or exists only in calm markets.

**Sector agent** — Attribution by GICS sector and market cap bucket. Identifies where your edge is concentrated and where you have no business allocating capital.

**Hold duration agent** — Maps conviction to outcomes across eight time brackets. Flags high-churn portfolios where transaction friction is quietly eating returns.

**Luck vs skill agent** — Computes estimated alpha against a passive SPY baseline, measures year-over-year consistency, and checks whether total P&L is concentrated in a handful of outlier trades.

After all six agents complete, a language model synthesizes every finding into a single narrative edge report — a verdict, not a dashboard.

## Data Sources

All free, no paid tiers required.

- **yfinance** — sector, industry, market cap per ticker
- **FRED** — VIX history, federal funds rate, recession period indicators
- **SPY price history** — market regime classification via 50d/200d SMA
- **Groq (Llama 3.3 70B)** — narrative synthesis, free inference tier

---

## Supported Brokers

Robinhood, Interactive Brokers (IBKR), Schwab, Fidelity. Any broker that exports a standard transaction history CSV can be added by extending the parser.
